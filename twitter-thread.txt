TWEET 2:
--------
1/ Prompt Bisect üîç

Your AI started giving weird answers. Which version broke it?

pit bisect start --failing-input "why is the sky blue?"
pit bisect good v1
pit bisect bad v50

Binary search finds the culprit. Minutes, not hours.

3/ Shareable Patches üì¶
